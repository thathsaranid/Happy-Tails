What is the Happy Tail?


Happy tail is an organization committed to regulating the population and eliminating rabies through nationwide sterilization and vaccination programs, while also enhancing the welfare of street dogs through its rescue and rehoming operations. Although we do our best to reach as many peoples as we can in other regions, the majority of care for the injured are concentrated in the Sri Lank province. Happy Tail has been at the Vanguard, influencing animal legislation and playing a crucial part in significantly improving the lives of both people and animals.
